VESC Mission

Our open source technology allows for creative minds from all over the world to collaborate on and engineer the smoothest, safest, and gnarliest one wheeled hyper core personal electronic vehicle. Our version of a VESC, otherwise known as "Vedder Electronic Speed Controller" can optimize the current shell of a stock "Onewheel" device. Alternatively DIYers can buy or make their own parts required to hold our pre built VESC components.

DIY Resources

View all the necessary components required to DIY your VESC board. We offer video instructional, documentation, and links to forums with helpful information to ensure you have a successful, safe VESC installation.
